About Us

MyGlucoGuide is an AI-powered health information and prediction platform focused on diabetes awareness, prevention, and personalized insights. We aim to help users understand the causes, types, and management of diabetes while also predicting their potential risk based on factors like age, gender, BMI, lifestyle, and family history.

Who We Are

We are a team of health enthusiasts, data scientists, and developers dedicated to bridging the gap between technology and preventive healthcare. Our mission is to empower individuals with data-driven insights that help them take control of their health.
